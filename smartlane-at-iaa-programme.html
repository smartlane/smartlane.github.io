SMARTLANE at the IAA this week

By Mathias Baur and Matthew Fullerton
Di 15 September 2015

The IAA is probably the most important automotive show in the world, and we are there as part of the "New Mobility World" exhibition. You can find us at Stand A21 in Hall 3.1. During the week we'll be presenting SMARTLANE in various ways, it would be great to see you there:

  • Tue. 15th Sept. at 1:30pm in the Startup Zone - 90s Startup Pitch - Hear Monja tell you in just 90s how SMARTLANE can transform your business.
  • Tue. 15th Sept. at 5.30pm in the Multimodal Market Hall - Technology & Big Data - Brief Info - Hear Florian give a short talk all about SMARTLANE and what it can offer to improve your mobility management.
  • Wed. 16th Sept. at 9:30am in the Startup Zone - 90s Startup Pitch (Press Conference) - Monja pitches to the press what SMARTLANE has to offer
  • Thu. 17th Sept. at 4:30pm in the Startup Zone - (TBC) Pitch (Making Cities smarter) - How can SMARTLANE make cities smarter? We'll tell you how (note: to be confirmed).<
  • Fri. 18th Sept. at 5:00pm in the Multimodal Market Hall - Sustainable Mobility through digital solutions (hosted by German Partnership for Sustainable Mobility) - Florian will join Innoz, Moovel and Ally on a discussion panel exploring how digital solutions can make mobility more sustainable
  • Wed. 23rd Sept. at 3:00pm in the Multimodal Market Hall - Big Data supporting Mobility - Mathias will talk about the relevancy of big data in today's mobility management.

It's all a lot to keep track of (even for us!), so we've put together an ICAL file which you can import into your calendar so you'll not miss anything.
