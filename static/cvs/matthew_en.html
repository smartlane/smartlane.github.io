After working more with driving and traffic simulation data I moved into a more general role as a freelance software developer, focusing on web and mobile development, sensor data capture, data analysis and visualisation. From 2015 onwards Smartlane has been taking most of my attention as we seek to build a platform along with light-weight apps for making mobility and logistics more efficient.
