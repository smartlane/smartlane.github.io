When I finished my computer science degree I started working as a software engineer at the renowned German software company sd&m. After one year, I jumped at the chance to take responsibility for the simulation based impact assessment of modern connected vehicular systems at Technische Universität München. I finished this high-profile research with a PhD in a related topic. Building on several methods and technologies developed during this research period, I connected with Matthew Fullerton and Florian Schimandl for the eventually successful application for a business startup grant. During the grant period, we incorporated the Smartlane UG (haftungsbeschränkt/Limited Liability) for the purpose of promoting an intelligent web-based dispatching software for modern On-Demand logistics.
