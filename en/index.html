Mobility Intelligence

With SMARTLANE Mobility Intelligence you have access to a performant and straightforward web-based platform. This can generate new insights and predictions from your own mobility information, enhanced with valuable additional sources that we collect and integrate for you.

Data integration

SMARTLANE opens up your data silos and allows you to create a secure, accessible and integrated data hub. In this way your own data are carefully combined with external sources in order to provide comprehensive information value.

Machine Learning

SMARTLANE allows you to learn from your data and reveals mobility-related challenges and their repercussions. SMARTLANE Mobility Intelligence uses innovative learning algorithms to make early and precise predictions.

Visualisierte Einblicke

Erleben Sie inno­vative Echt­zeit­visualisier­ungen der Erkennt­nisse aus Ihren Daten. Behalt­en Sie mit unseren über­sicht­lich­en Dashboards stets den Überblick über die aus­sage­kräftigst­en Indikat­or­en Ihrer Mobilität.
