Smartlane - IT for tomorrow's mobility

Concentrate on what you do well and let us support you with innovative, data-driven solutions for your mobility, logistic and location-centric needs.


Smartlane's team brings together many years of experience in research and practice in both mobility technology and IT. We offer development and consulting expertise in the following areas:
 

Analysis of big geodata in local and distributed environments

Interactive map-based web and mobile applications

Driver and passenger assistance, traffic control and routing services

Data-driven applications in SQL-/NoSQL and big data environments

Java, Python and Node.js software development

Team

Florian Schimandl

  

Project management, Data modelling, Customer support


Florian is a computer scientist with many years of experience in data architectures, modelling and simulation in traffic and mobility. After graduating from TU München he worked as a research assistant and group leader at the Chair of Traffic Engineering and Control at TUM. His doctoral thesis (under preparation) tackles the analysis and architecture of cooperative systems and the relevant data quality.

Dr. Mathias Baur

  

Product development, data- and software architecture


Mathias is a specialist in professional software architectures and for mobility data-based model development. He has many years experience from industry, research and as a partner in an earlier IT company. In his doctoral thesis he quantitatively evaluated the potential for improvement in road traffic through simulation- and data-based optimization of driver assistance and traffic control systems

Matthew Fullerton

Software development, System administration, Research coordination


Matthew has been developing software and performing research in the UK, USA and Germany over the last 10 years as both an employee and freelancer. He has worked extensively with transportation system modelling, open data harmonization, sensor communication, web page crawling and scraping, image processing and is also good with IT infrastructure building and maintenance.

Monja Egbers

  

Business development and Sales


Monja received her MSc. in Management and Technology (TUM-BWL) from TUM, Munich. She is responsible for SMARTLANE’s sales, marketing and financing and has a wide range of practical experience including consulting in the automotive industry, sales and product strategy development in the early phases for startups (Masters Thesis research) as well as in managerial accounting at a German insurance company.
