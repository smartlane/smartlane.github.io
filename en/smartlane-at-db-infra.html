SMARTLANE takes part in DB Infrastructure Accelerator Challenge

By Matthew Fullerton and Monja Egbers
Di 14 Juli 2015

DB are looking for innovation in their infrastructure management and are running a 3 month accelerator program over the summer in the Betahaus in Berlin. Out of 120 applications, SMARTLANE was among 11 contestants chosen to take part in the pitch event on June 23rd.
